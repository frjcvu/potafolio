BIOGRAPHY

My Story

My name is Frida Camila Jorge Aldaco and I am 18 years old. I was born and raised in the city of Irapuato, in Guanajuato, Mexico. I am currently in the sixth semester of high school at Colegio Interamericano. Since I joined this institution, I have had the opportunity to experience enriching educational moments that have strengthened my love for learning and my desire for self-improvement.

My Passion

I am especially interested in the world of mechanical and electrical engineering and how its application can improve our daily lives. I’m passionate about understanding how systems, machines, and processes work, as well as finding creative solutions to technical problems.

My Approach

I want to combine technological innovation with social and environmental responsibility. I’m motivated to develop projects that contribute to industrial progress, always maintaining an ethical, sustainable, and human-centered perspective.

My Vision

I dream of studying mechanical and electrical engineering at a reputable university. I would like to specialize in the industrial field, learning about mechanical systems design, automation, and manufacturing so I can contribute to the technological and economic development of the state—especially considering the industrial potential due to the strategic location of our city.
